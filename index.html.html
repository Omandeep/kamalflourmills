Our Story

Rooted in Punjab's
Farming Heritage

Kamal Flour Mills was founded in 1982 in the heartland of Punjab's wheat belt. Today, under the stewardship of Sh. Kamal Kumar, we proudly serve thousands of families across Mardanheri, Samana and Patiala district with fresh, natural flour and milling services.

1982
Unit founded by the family. First stone chakki installed in Village Mardanheri.
1991
Sh. Kamal Kumar takes ownership. Expands with oil expeller and feed grinding.
2000s
Major machinery upgradation. Rice milling and masala grinding added. 35 KVA power load secured.
2024–26
PMFME scheme modernisation — automated atta line, modern shed, packaged brand launch.
